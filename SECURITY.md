# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

NONE - SPACER IS STILL IN PRODUCTION!
DO NOT USE FOR PERSONAL USE!

## Reporting a Vulnerability

1. **Do not disclose the issue publicly.**
   - Please do not post the issue on GitHub or other public forums until I have had a chance to review and address it.

2. **Submit your report.**
   - Send an email to eccywebsitedev@proton.me with a detailed description of the vulnerability.
   - Include steps to reproduce the issue, information about the environment, and any other relevant details.

3. **Fix and Release.**
   - Once the vulnerability is confirmed, I will work on a fix.
   - A security release will be coordinated, and you will be credited for your responsible disclosure.

## Guidelines

- Please provide detailed information to help me understand and reproduce the issue.
- Allow a reasonable amount of time to address the issue before disclosing it to the public.
- Do not exploit the vulnerability for any reason other than to demonstrate its existence.
